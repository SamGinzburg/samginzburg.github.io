Biography

Hi, I'm a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Princeton University, advised by Prof. Michael Freedman in the SNS Research Group. I am primarily interested in improving the cost and efficiency of cloud computing, but I am also interested in compilers, operating systems, virtualization, and distributed systems. My research interests also extend to the intersection of systems and programming languages research.

I previously worked in the PLASMA Research Group at the University of Massachusetts - Amherst, with Prof. Emery Berger, as well as the UConn Plant Computational Genomic Lab with Prof. Jill Wegrzyn.
